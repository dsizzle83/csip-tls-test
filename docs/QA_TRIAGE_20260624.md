## Failures grouped by root cause

### Cluster A — Closed-loop actuation gap (open-loop "ACK = applied")
The hub writes a setpoint and assumes it took effect; it verifies measured convergence only
on the **solar gen-limit** lever, and even that isn't on the bench.

- **FAIL `reject-write-curtail`**, **FAIL `enable-gate-curtail`** — solar ACKs curtailment,
  output stays 3650 W over the 1000 W cap for 49 s, no CannotComply.
  *Root:* `checkGenLimitConvergence` (HEAD) covers exactly this → **likely just undeployed.**
- **FAIL `ev-accept-but-ignore`** — EV ACKs SetChargingProfile, draws 1430 W over a 0 W
  import cap for 69 s. *Root:* **no EV measured-draw convergence in HEAD** — genuinely unfixed.
  `applyCommand` trusts the OCPP "Accepted"; optimizer never compares commanded vs measured EV W.
- **FAIL `battery-charge-disabled`** — under 0-export cap at full sun the hub commands the
  battery to absorb; pack silently refuses; hub exports 4400 W for 50 s, empty plan log, no
  fallback to PV curtailment, no CannotComply. *Root:* **no battery measured-absorb
  verification + no lever fallback** — genuinely unfixed.

### Cluster B — Telemetry plausibility / staleness (trusts garbage)
- **FAIL `solar-bad-scale`** — corrupted W_SF makes the hub read 48 000 W for a 4.8 kW
  inverter on 33/35 samples. *Root:* **no nameplate sanity-gate on decoded SunSpec power.**
  `derbase.go:36` has `Wmax` but it isn't applied as a read-path clamp. **LOCKSTEP** — sunspec
  maps mirror into csip-tls-test.
- **FAIL `ev-wrong-units`** — charger reports current in mA under an "A" label; hub surfaces
  6000 A against a 32 A station on 31/40 samples. *Root:* `applySamplesLocked`
  (`cmd/ocpp/main.go:349`) copies `CurrentImport` straight into `s.currentA` with **no
  validation against the station `MaxCurrentA`** (which it already knows, line 190).
- **BLIND `stale-meter`** — grid meter frozen at 327 670 W, hub keeps trusting it.

## Fix plan (in lexa-hub unless noted)

**Step 0 — Confirm & deploy HEAD, then re-run (BLOCKING).**
Rebuild lexa-hub arm64, deploy to 69.0.0.1, restart, then:
`scripts/mayhem.py --only reject-write-curtail,enable-gate-curtail,clock-jitter,stale-meter,ev-meter-freeze`
Expected: these flip to PASS/DEGRADED. Whatever stays FAIL gets reclassified as "fix
insufficient" and folded into the steps below. *(Requires SSH/deploy approval — currently
blocked for me.)*

**Step 1 — Generalize closed-loop convergence to battery + EV levers** (Cluster A residue).
Mirror `checkGenLimitConvergence` for the other two actuators in `optimizer.go`:
- battery: compare measured absorb (−PowerW) vs commanded charge; on sustained no-absorb,
  escalate to PV curtailment, else record breach → CannotComply.
- EV: compare measured draw vs commanded current; on no-effect, escalate lever / suspend the
  transaction, else CannotComply.
Fixes `battery-charge-disabled`, `ev-accept-but-ignore`.

**Step 2 — Telemetry plausibility validation** (Cluster B FAILs).
- SunSpec: clamp/flag decoded active power against `Wmax` on the read path
  (`internal/southbound/...`). **LOCKSTEP** ship the same decode guard in csip-tls-test
  `internal/southbound/sunspec`. Fixes `solar-bad-scale`.
- OCPP: in `applySamplesLocked`, reject/clamp `CurrentImport` (and check unit/multiplier)
  against the station `MaxCurrentA`. Fixes `ev-wrong-units`.

**Step 3 — CSIP malformed-resource fail-CLOSED** (Cluster C FAILs).
- Retain last-known-good `ActiveControl` when `Evaluate` would otherwise return nil for an
  empty/malformed program list (don't drop an adopted cap).
- Bound/validate control limits (reject overflow / > nameplate) before adoption.
Fixes `malform-empty-program`, `malform-huge-activepower`; hardens the DEGRADED leaf cases.

**Step 4 — Per-EVSE MeterValues freshness** (Cluster B BLIND).
Stamp each EVSE's last MeterValues time; stale-expire a silent charger and surface it (keep
cap compliance on the grid meter). Fixes `ev-meter-freeze`.

**Step 5 — Battery direction verification** (Cluster E).
Verify measured battery direction/SoC-trend vs commanded sign; halt/alarm on divergence.
Also bump the scenario setup SoC to ≥12% (harness, `cmd/dashboard/mayhem.go`) to drop the
incidental floor noise. Fixes `battery-wrong-sign`.

After each step, re-run `scripts/mayhem.py --only <ids>` and confirm the verdict flips.
